\begin{abstract}
The need of integration of different fields of geoscience is valuable and important for the petroleum exploration and production. We analyze the different taxonomies, standards and ontologies that have been developed for the area of geoscience in order to find similarities concepts between models to establish the way we can integrate them. In addition, this review will describe the fundamental concepts of ontologies, geology and detailed description of each geology ontology. 
\end{abstract}
